The 1-get-data.ipynb uses the Google Earth Engine.
To request task to the Earth Engine, an account and project is required in its platform.

dubai_regions.geojson is prepared using the Earh Engine Editor.
After manual labeling of the regions, the geojson values are exported from the editor.

Using the geojson and Earth Engine access, 1-get-data.ipynb submits tasks.
The tasks can be seens from : https://code.earthengine.google.com/
Outputs of the tasks then exported to Google Drive by the Engine.
raw_weekly_indexes are the those results donwloaded from the Drive.


# the submitted tasks can take up an hour to execute
# the export will be sent to drive by default
